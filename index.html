The Process

How It Works

From job intake to payment in four simple steps. RIN automates everything so you focus on driving.

1

Job Comes In

Customer or enterprise client submits a roadside request. Location, vehicle type, incident — all in RIN.

2

AI Prices It

RIN calculates pricing based on incident type, distance, and time. Customer approves via SMS.

3

Driver Matched

Nearest available, qualified driver gets the offer. One text to confirm. No app needed.

4

Get Paid

Job done. Customer confirms. Payment captured. Your 80% tracked automatically in your portal.
