How It Works

From dispatch to payment — automated

RIN handles customer intake, pricing, and confirmation. Your driver gets a clean job with an approved price.

1

Job comes in

Customer or enterprise client submits a roadside request — location, vehicle, incident type.

2

AI prices the job

RIN calculates a fair price based on incident type, distance, time of day. Customer approves via SMS.

3

Your driver is matched

The nearest available, qualified driver from your fleet gets the offer. Confirm by SMS — no app needed.

4

Complete & get paid

Driver marks job done. Customer confirms. Payment is captured. Your 80% is tracked automatically in your portal.
