Common questions

Before you call.

Does RIN touch my MTO contract or my direct jobs?
No. RIN only handles overflow — jobs your truck cannot take because it's already running. Your MTO contract rates, your government relationship, your direct customers: all untouched. RIN operates in the gap.
What's the commission structure?
0% on jobs you run with your own drivers. 20% on jobs dispatched to the RIN pool — covering dispatch, billing, payment processing, and 7-day driver payout. No monthly fee. No setup cost.
We already use WhatsApp for dispatch. Is this compatible?
RIN replaces the WhatsApp dispatch group — not your drivers. Your team gets jobs by SMS, the same way they do now. No new app. No retraining. The change is invisible to your drivers.
What does billing look like at month end?
One line per job: date, route, rate, your cut, RIN fee. Your own-driver jobs show 0% commission. Pool jobs show 20%. No disputed line items. No invoices to chase. Clean record from day one.
