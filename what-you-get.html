RIN is open to independent operators of any size. Here's what you need to qualify.

📄

Valid CVOR or equivalent

You must hold a current commercial vehicle operating record or equivalent provincial license for your tow class.

🛡️

Minimum $2M liability insurance

Standard commercial tow insurance covering your operation. We'll ask for a certificate of insurance during onboarding.

🚚

At least one operational tow truck

Any class — flatbed, wheel-lift, heavy. 1 truck is enough to get started. No fleet minimum.

📍

GTA service coverage

You need to cover at least part of the Greater Toronto Area. No exclusivity required — keep your existing clients and accounts.

📱

A phone that can receive SMS

That's it for tech. Your drivers confirm jobs by text. No smartphone required. No app to install.

⚠️

Good standing — no active complaints

RIN does a basic background check on your business. Active TSSA complaints or unresolved consumer disputes may delay onboarding.

Questions

The ones operators always ask

If your question isn't here, call us.

Do I have to give up my current clients or contracts? +
No. RIN adds volume to your operation — it doesn't replace it. Keep every existing client, motor club contract, or private call. RIN is additive, not exclusive.
What if I don't want to accept a job that comes in? +
You can decline. RIN will move to the next available operator. There's no penalty for occasional declines. Consistently declining jobs may affect your dispatch priority — we'll tell you if that happens.
How exactly does payment work? +
When your driver marks a job complete, RIN logs it. Within 7 days, 80% of the job total is deposited directly to your registered bank account via EFT. RIN invoices the client separately — you never have to think about collections.
What if there's a dispute about a job? +
Every job is logged with GPS tracking, timestamps, driver ID, and customer sign-off. If a client disputes a charge, RIN handles the resolution — not you. Your payment isn't withheld during a dispute unless fraud is suspected.
Is the 20% fee negotiable? +
Not for standard membership. High-volume operators (50+ jobs/month) can discuss volume pricing with our team. The 20% covers dispatch, billing, collections, platform, and the 7-day payment guarantee.
What kinds of jobs come through RIN? +
Insurance roadside (lockouts, tows, tire changes, fuel delivery), fleet breakdowns, and dealer transport jobs. All jobs are pre-qualified — no cash calls, no unknown clients. Rates are set before dispatch.
Can I add more drivers or trucks later? +
Yes. Just let us know and we'll register additional drivers in the system. There's no limit on fleet size and no additional cost to add drivers.
